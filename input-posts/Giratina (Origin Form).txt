##EB basics

This is a 300 stages **time-based Ghost** type challenge, that will last for 14 days. Super Effective types you can use against are Ghost and Dark.

Drop rates are **25/25/25** for all stages.

- Skill 1: Sinister Power – Requires 120 PSB to max. Unfortunately not being Dark type greatly hinders the viability ad the skill.
- Skill 2 (SS): Cross Attack + – Requires 120 PSB to max. Nice skill, but not a priority considering the great alternatives of Ghost types.

Hearts Left|Skip Chance
:-:|:-:

0|30%
